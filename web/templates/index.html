MUSIC GENRE
CLASSIFIER

This site uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to predict the genre of any song it hears.

Instructions: Using the box to the left, Drag and Drop or Click to upload a .WAV audio file. The file will be uploaded to our server and run through the CNN model. When the model is done making its prediction, you will see a graph of the model's average confidence across 10 musical genres. The top confidence will be the model's best prediction for the songs genre!

Tips: Try to keep the size of your uploaded .WAV file beneath 10 MB. You can use a free software like Audacity to trim your song down to size!
